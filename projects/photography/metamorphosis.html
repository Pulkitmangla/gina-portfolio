Metamorphosis

- January 2016 -

The piece illustrates the attempts to escape the many forms of constraints, ranging from the mind, body, spirit, and environment, through the use of the human form within architecture. The work also acts as means of metamorphosis with the constraints we have as humans. The photos may serve as a reminder that we are not always capable of escaping the constraints of our environment or society.
